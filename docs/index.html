About Me

Welcome to my website!

I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Data Science at Western Washington University, along with a minor in Anthropology. My main focus is on Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Data Modeling, with some additional experience in Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision.

I've applied myself to a range of team-based projects involving large datasets, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling. One of my proudest accomplishments was initiating a partnership between Western Washington University and Woodland Park Zoo to develop a machine learning system for non-invasive animal behavior tracking. I've also worked with U.S. geological data, Washington State education data, and quality control chemical datasets, and I feel each project has pushed my skills and grown my knowledge. I work well both independently and in groups, and I take pride in being organized, communicative, and easy to collaborate with. I’ve taken on leadership roles in client-facing projects, helping ensure clear communication, effective problem-solving, and timely delivery.

Feel free to reach out if you’re interested in collaborating, chatting, or sharing opportunities! I’m always open to learning and making new connections.
